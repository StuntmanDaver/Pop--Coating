# Pop's Industrial Coatings — Product Context

## Product Purpose

Marketing website for Pop's Industrial Coatings, a 50+ year family-owned industrial finishing shop in Lakeland, FL. The site's job is to convert procurement officers, plant managers, and operations directors from B2B industrial companies (aerospace, defense, heavy equipment, agriculture) into phone calls and quote requests.

## Register

brand — this is a marketing/lead-gen site where the design IS the product

## Users

**Primary:** Procurement officers and plant managers at manufacturing companies within 100-mile radius of Lakeland, FL. They Google vendors before making a call. They evaluate credibility through: professional appearance, certifications listed, capacity/equipment mentioned, longevity signals.

**Secondary:** Existing customers checking hours, phone, or needing a quote for a new project.

**Tertiary:** Job seekers and referral partners.

## Brand

- **Name:** Pop's Industrial Coatings
- **Founded:** 1972 — 50+ years in operation, 4 generations
- **Location:** Lakeland, FL (Polk County)
- **Tone:** Authoritative, family-built, precise. Not corporate-cold. Not casual-friendly. Think a contractor who has seen everything and will get it right the first time.
- **Anti-references:** Generic contractor websites with stock photos and clip art. Consumer-friendly coating chains. Anything that looks cheap or templated.

## Services

1. Wet Paint Coatings
2. Complex Coating
3. Abrasive Media Blasting
4. Powder Coating
5. Large Capacity Powder Coating

## Key Differentiators

- 50+ years / 4 generations (trust + longevity)
- Large-capacity equipment (handles biggest industrial projects)
- Aerospace and defense clientele
- Family ownership = accountability
- Lakeland FL location serving entire Polk County

## Strategic Principles

- Lead with trust (age, generations, certifications) before services
- Make "Request a Quote" the easiest action on every page
- Photography should show real work, real facility — not stock
- Every page should reinforce: professional, capable, reliable

## Success Metrics

- Phone calls / quote form submissions
- Time on page (engagement)
- Bounce rate from Google (first impression quality)
